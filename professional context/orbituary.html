In Loving Memory

Djimy Francillon

"Those we love remain with us, for love itself lives on."

Djimy Francillon was born on August 20, 1992, in Port-au-Prince, Haiti. He was the beloved son of Jacqueline Michel and Isidor Francillon, who preceded him in death in 2005 and 2002, respectively.

On October 22, 2022, Djimy married the love of his life, Vela Sanon, a beautiful and devoted partner. Together, they shared a life filled with love, ambition, and purpose. They are blessed with a daughter, Djessie Aela Francillon, who brought immense joy to their family.

In 2018, Djimy earned a bachelor’s degree in Chemical Engineering, followed by certifications in Data Science in 2019. His professional journey began in September 2017 as a teacher of mathematics and physics at Interfamilia’s College. In 2019, he joined Unigestion Holding S.A as an intern and later became a Fraud Analyst.

Djimy had a passion for learning and facing new challenges. He loved listening to music, playing soccer, and spending quality time playing games with friends and family. His joy, curiosity, and resilience inspired those who knew him.

He and his family resided in Pétion-Ville, Haiti, where he worked hard toward a better future. His goal was to earn a bachelor’s degree in Software Development, secure a meaningful career, and find a peaceful place to raise his wife and daughter amidst Haiti’s political, economic, and social instability.

Forever remembered, forever missed. May you rest in peace, Djimy.
